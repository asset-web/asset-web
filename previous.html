About Content Management Systems

A CMS is software, usually a database driven application, that will make it easy to create and maintain websites and manage all publishing formats associated with the World Wide Web.

Key Features:

  • Separation of content, structure and design
  • Easy content production without the need for programming

Additional features may include:

  • Workflow system, to manage content scheduling and approval
  • Templating, to provide a consistent look and feel for particular content types and throughout the web site
